How does the clock work?

The clock uses pomodoro functionality. Pomodoro is a technique with purpose to improve productivity mesured by a timer. Work or study time is getting split into smaller chanks with breaks taken in between. For example, you can have 4 work sessions of 20 minutes each with 5 minutes of break taken after every session.
